Policies

Please review before booking.

These policies help protect appointment time, keep scheduling smooth, and make sure every client has the best experience possible.

Deposits

  • A $25 nonrefundable deposit is required to secure your appointment.
  • Your appointment is not confirmed until the deposit is received.

Preparation

  • Please come detangled to help us stay on schedule.
  • Arrive ready for your selected service unless otherwise discussed.

Hair included

  • Hair is included for braiding styles in natural colors 1, 1B, 2, and 4 only.
  • For other colors, clients should bring their own hair unless pre-approved.

Late arrivals

  • Please arrive on time for your scheduled appointment.
  • Late arrivals may need to be rescheduled depending on the service and timing.

Cancellations

  • If you need to cancel or reschedule, please do so with as much notice as possible.
  • Deposits remain nonrefundable after booking.

Questions

  • Text only if you have questions before your appointment.
  • For booking requests, please use the booking page.
