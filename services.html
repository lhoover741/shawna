Add-ons & booking logic

What changes based on the service you select.

Included hair only shows for eligible braiding styles

During booking, the included-hair field appears for knotless, stitch braids, braided ponytail, lemonade braids, and Fulani braids. Natural colors only: 1, 1B, 2, and 4.

Add-ons change by service

Not every add-on appears for every style. Boho, past waist length, crimps, sew-in take down, and the stitch-braid no-weave option are shown only when they apply to the service selected in the form.

Booking form controls the final request details

The service page gives you the menu. The booking page gives you the exact options that apply to your appointment, including same-day approval notes, time limits, and deposit reminders.

Before you book
  • Appointments are available Tuesday through Saturday, 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM.
  • We are closed Sunday and Monday.
  • Same-day bookings are allowed only if approved.
  • A $25 nonrefundable deposit is due after approval to secure your appointment.
  • Please come detangled to help us stay on schedule.
