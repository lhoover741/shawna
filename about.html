About Your Stylist

Hi, I’m Shawna.

I’ve been styling hair since 2016, starting by practicing on friends and family and building my skills through hands-on experience with braids, ponytails, quick weaves, and more.

My focus is clean, consistent, polished work.

I graduated from cosmetology school in 2023 and continue to focus on improving consistency, clean parts, and finished results that feel professional. Ravishing Beauté is built around care, detail, and making every appointment feel intentional.

My goal is for each client to leave with a style that looks neat, feels flattering, and reflects the standard I want my work to be known for.
