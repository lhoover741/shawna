About Your Stylist

Hi, I’m Shawna.

Hi, I’m Shawna — the stylist behind Ravishing Beautè. I’ve been building my skills and confidence since 2016, starting with friends and family and growing through hands-on practice with braids, ponytails, and quick-weaves.

My story

After graduating cosmetology school in 2023, I focused on consistency, clean parts, and healthy-looking finishes. My goal is simple: styles that look good, feel good, and last.

At Ravishing Beautè, I care about a polished experience from start to finish. Whether you book braids, a quick weave, a sew-in, or a sleek ponytail, I want you to leave feeling confident, comfortable, and proud of your style.

My approach
Clean parts, beautiful finishes, and styles that leave you feeling as good as you look.
