About your stylist

I am Shawna.

I believe great hair starts with care, professionalism, and attention to detail. Every appointment is a chance to help you feel more confident when you walk out than when you walked in.

Versatile stylist Started in 2016 Cosmetology grad Northwest Indiana

My story

I have been styling since freshman year of high school, starting with friends and family and learning through practice, patience, and passion. What began with ponytails and braid styles turned into something much bigger. After graduating cosmetology school in 2023, I committed fully to sharpening my craft and building a beauty experience clients can trust.

I care about neat parts, balanced looks, and making sure your style feels finished from every angle. Whether you book braids, a quick weave, a sew-in, or a sleek ponytail, I want you to feel like your appointment was worth it from start to finish.

My approach
Beautiful work is important, but so is how you feel while you are in my chair.
