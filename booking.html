Booking

A cleaner booking flow from start to finish.

Choose your service, request a date and start time during booking hours, add your style details, and send your request directly through the site. We review every request first, and same-day appointments still require approval.

Start booking View prices
Step 1

Pick your style

Use the grouped service menu so it is easier to find knotless braids, stitch braids, ponytails, quickweaves, and sew-ins.

Step 2

Add your details

Choose a date that falls Tuesday through Saturday, request a start time during business hours, then add your hair details and any matching add-ons.

Step 3

Wait for approval

Your request is reviewed before it is confirmed. A $25 nonrefundable deposit is due only after we approve the appointment, and same-day requests stay pending until approved in the admin dashboard.
